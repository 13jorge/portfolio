The Ride, Video Art, 2019

I wanted to create a video where I show the viewer what I do throughout the week. I used those videos that I recorded and made an art video that could be fun to watch.

The Beeping Current, Sound Art, 2019

I walked around San Jose State campus and listened for noises that sounded interesting. I recorded those noise and put them together on audacity and create a very unique sound.

Controller Ghost, Glitch Art, 2019

My laptop had a very interesting desktop image and since my laptop keyboard I wanted to see how it would looked like if I glitched it. I took a picture of my laptop and started to glitch it and a very interesting glitched image come out of it.

The Spartan Wonderland, Composite Art, 2019

My concept for this project was to take San Jose State to another world. So I decided to makes things that were not really real or things that usually are not together. That where the volcano and water come into place, as well as the big foxes.

Gaming Memories, Net Art, 2019

Growing up most of my memories were playing on consoles mainly this characters you seen here. They were the things that kept me distracted and I remember most from my childhood. This is an attempt to recreate that game into net art.

Red Eyes, Code Art , 2019

For this art I wanted to create something that was imaginary but had read eyes. As I kept working on it I decided I wanted the user to create their own, there for in made squared follow the mouse to see what the user could create.

The Hidden Black Square, Net Art, 2019

I wanted to create a game that was in a way hard but at the same time colorful. I came up with this concept because looking for something is hard especially when it blends in with the background. This a challenging game that but can help with memory coordination. Can you Find the Black Square?
